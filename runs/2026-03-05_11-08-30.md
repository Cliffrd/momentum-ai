## Analysis

**KEY_THEMES:**

- **Export functionality is broken for real workflows** — Sarah's 2-hour Monday ritual + 23 tickets about row limits + 41 requests for CSV improvements all point to exports that don't match how customers actually use their data
- **User management is a hidden churn risk** — 31 "how do I add a user" tickets + Sarah's permissions mess + 38 bulk management requests signal that team administration is unintuitive and creating friction at scale
- **Onboarding isn't surfacing core value** — James nearly churned because he didn't discover bulk actions for 3 weeks; 22 requests for onboarding checklist confirms we're leaving users to figure it out alone
- **HubSpot integration is the most-requested capability by a wide margin** — 67 Intercom requests + 44 support tickets asking about it; this is table stakes for a segment we're clearly attracting
- **Performance/reliability issues eroding trust** — 18 Firefox tickets + slow dashboards suggest technical debt that's becoming visible to customers

**OPPORTUNITIES:**

- **Fix export to handle enterprise reporting workflows** — Sarah represents a segment doing recurring, formatted exports; solving this protects $XX,XXX ARR accounts — *Medium effort*
- **Build HubSpot integration** — Highest raw signal volume, likely blocking deals and expansions; validates we're in a CRM-adjacent workflow — *High effort*
- **Redesign user management with bulk actions and clearer permissions** — Pain spans SMB (James) to Enterprise (Sarah); directly impacts expansion revenue — *Medium effort*
- **Add progressive onboarding that surfaces bulk actions and key features** — James almost churned; this is silent revenue loss we can't measure — *Low effort*
- **Remove 1000-row export limit or make it transparent** — 23 tickets = probably 100+ frustrated users who didn't write in — *Low effort*

**HYPOTHESIS:**

"If we build scheduled, formatted exports with templates, then enterprise operations users (like Sarah) will eliminate their manual Monday reporting workflow, because they're currently spending 2+ hours translating our output into their boss's required format."

**PRD_OUTLINE:**

**Problem:** Enterprise customers are spending hours manually reformatting exports because our export tool outputs raw data, not workflow-ready reports.

**Target User:** Operations leads at 100+ seat accounts who produce recurring reports for leadership.

**Success Metrics:**
- Reduce average export-to-deliverable time by 75% (survey sample)
- Decrease export-related support tickets by 50% within 60 days

**Proposed Solution:**
- Saved export templates with custom column ordering, naming, and formatting
- Scheduled exports (daily/weekly/monthly) delivered via email or webhook
- Remove or increase the 1000-row limit to 50K with async processing for large jobs

**Open Question:** Do we need to support Excel-native formatting (.xlsx with formulas/styling), or is CSV with correct structure sufficient for 80% of use cases?

## TL;DR

**Remove the 1000-row export limit immediately**—it's low effort, high signal (23 tickets = ~100+ silent frustrated users), and buys you goodwill and data on export usage patterns before you invest in the larger templating system.
